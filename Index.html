What is Navjeevan AI?

Navjeevan AI is a health-centric artificial intelligence platform designed to support expectant mothers and healthcare providers. It leverages advanced machine learning to assess pregnancy risk, predict fetal health conditions, and offer intelligent assistance in real-time.

Our AI Capabilities

Pregnancy Risk Prediction

Get AI-driven insights into potential pregnancy complications based on clinical data and symptoms.

Fetal Health Prediction

Analyze fetal heart rate and other indicators to predict potential health concerns early.

AI Assistant

A chatbot-style assistant to answer questions, provide suggestions, and guide through pregnancy stages.
